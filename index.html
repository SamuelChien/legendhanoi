Introduce

We believe that the beauty of life is the environment around us, where living with good living environment creates the beauty of life. The Legend is a well-designed apartment project for modern urbanites who enjoy an integrated lifestyle of work, family and entertainment in a convenient transportation location.

Welcome home.

Community

At the very core of THE LEGEND is a comprehensive collection of specialty purpose rooms, furnishings, equipment and technology programmed to add convenience and utility everyday - for live, work and play. With in-building amenities THE LEGEND residents enjoy a quick vertical commute and the luxury of time.

Seize the day.

Living the Dream

Experience the unique blend of elegance, rich cultural lifestyle and refinement of a modern apartment building with light and wind filled to each apartment.

"Where life meets beauty!"

It’s nice to awake each day knowing that your home is programmed around your next move: Morning rendezvous with Fido and friends? Yoga class on the veranda? Breakfast in the lobby café? Executive team meeting in the boardroom? Grocery delivery at home? Catching the game in the media room with the fan club? Happy hour at the restaurant? Dry cleaning pick up at the Concierge. Private dining above it all? A night cap fireside in the lounge?

What’s your wish today?

Stacked up.
Downtown.

THE LEGEND isn’t just turning heads with its compelling architecture – it’s setting a new standard for urban living. A series of cubes offset by 8-degrees creating dynamic transition floors with unique amenities and residential terraces while a quick look inside reveals design features that are truly out of the box.

Distinctive Features

  • All homes reveal standards of luxury apartments including high standard aluminum windows, sound insulation glass
  • European standard systems for electricity and water supply, fire alarm, and security
  • Residence lobby air conditioning on par with 5 star hotel air conditioning
  • Located at a quiet and good security area in Trung Hoa Nhan Chinh District, Thanh Xuan Hanoi
  • Two basements for parking and three levels for high rise parking provide parking space for all 460 apartments
  • Dynamic retail experience with a grand shopping mall, a luxurious lobby, restaurant and coffee shop
  • Services include Four Seasons Pool, Spa, Gym, Restaurant, Coffee Standard
